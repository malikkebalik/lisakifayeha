    Career of Vyshonn King Miller

    Silkk’s career is broken into two categories: music and acting.

    After signing a deal with Priority in the mid-1990s, his music career took off. In August 1996, he released his first album, The Shocker. He was using the moniker Silkk at the time. He changed his name to Silkk the Shocker after the publication of The Shocker. Silkk was included on Master P’s Ghetto D album in 1997, but it was also released as a single in 1998.

    It Ain’t My Fault, which featured Mystikal, became Silkk the Shocker’s signature song. Silkk and Mya cooperated on the lead single from Silkk’s album Made Man in 1999. On the Billboard Charts, the song, titled Somebody Like Me, became one of the year’s best-selling singles. Silkk became a well-known mainstream artist in the late 1990s and early 2000s after his album Man-Made was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

    Silkk released My World, My Way in 2001. In its first week, the song sold over 200000 copies, and by July 2004, it had sold 712000 copies in the United States. Based on a True Story was released in 2004. The song did not receive the same amount of attention as he received in the late 1990s.

    Silkk freestyling over DJ Khaled’s All I Do is Win was put on YouTube on June 3, 2010. On August 21, 2010, he released his first mixtape, All I Do is Win. Silkk’s comeback to hip hop was signaled by the release of the mixtape. Silkk declared on his Twitter account on November 5, 2013, that he was still working on his album Incredible. Silkk released his first great album, Don’t Give Up, on April 24, 2014. On May 19, 2014, he released the second song from Incredible, We Ain’t Trippin. On September 4, 2015, his third single from Incredible, Business, was released. The Incredible album is still in the works for Silkk.
