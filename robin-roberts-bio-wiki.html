    Who is Robin Roberts?

    Robin Roberts is an American television broadcaster and guest reporter of ABC’s Good Morning America.

    She worked as a full-time co-anchor of the morning news program in 2005. She was awarded the 2012 Peabody Award.

    Robin Roberts: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

    She was born on November 23, 1960, in Tuskegee, Alabama. She grew up in Pass Christian, Mississippi. Her birth sign is Sagittarius.

    Robert is of American citizenship and her ethnicity is All-American. She is the youngest of four sisters.

    Her father’s name is Colonel Lawrence E. Roberts, a pilot and her mother’s name is Lucimarian Roberts.

    Her father was also a member of the Tuskegee Airmen.

    She spent her childhood practicing athletic and academic success.

    Robin Roberts also used to play basketball and tennis during her early years.

    Education, School/College University

    Robin Roberts completed her degree from Southeastern Louisiana University in 1983 where she studied with a scholarship in Journalism.

    Robin Roberts: Professional Life, Career

    She started her career in Mississippi as a sports reporter and anchored for a local TV station.

    She became successful and progressed to a larger city in the south.

    Robert was offered to work at ESPN and she hosted the news program, Sportscenter.

    In the meantime, she also made an appearance as a guest reporter on ABC’s Good Morning America.

    After analyzing her talent, ABC hired her as a full-time co-anchor of the morning news program.

    She along with George Stephanopoulos helped the anchor Diane Sawyer to lead the show to the top of the competitive morning show.

    Robin Roberts took a rest from her career in order to treat her rare blood disorder and she suffered from breast cancer in 2007.

    Bone marrow was transplanted to her in September.

    She made a come in her career on February 20, 2013, after the treatment i.e., after 174 days.

    She helped her show collect the best rating numbers with her show.

    Robin made her presence in ABC’s news coverage sporadically since then and also at the 2013 Academy Awards.

    But, she had not recovered fully and with the consult and suggestion of doctors, she anchored Good Morning America daily once again.

    Robin Roberts celebrated her 20th anniversary with Good Morning America in April 2022.

    Awards, Nominations
