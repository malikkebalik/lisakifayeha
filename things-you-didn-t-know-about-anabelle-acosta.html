    Anabelle Acosta is a famous American actress widely known for her starring in Ballers and Quantico. The actress was born on February 28, 1987, in Havana, Cuba.

    The actress herself is a beautiful lady with dark brown hair with her beautiful dark eyes. Along with that she has a fit body and is 5 feet 7 inches (170cm) tall. 

    Early Life and Education

    Acosta was born in a well-sophisticated family with a stable lifestyle so she did not have any difficulties regarding her early lifestyle and education. 

    Talking about her early education the actress attended Julliard School. There is no information regarding her university education but we do know that she took Meisner and method acting classes along with dance and on-stage training. 

    Family

    Although, Annabelle being in the limelight for quite some time she has not revealed any information regarding her parents out in the public.

    How Did Acosta Start Her Career?

    After finishing her education the present star started off her career by appearing in TV commercials and Ads. She made her entrance in her on-screen career back in the year 2008 when she was portrayed as a hot bartender in the movie "The Next Hit".

    After that, she has appeared in various movies and TV shows and has dedicated a lot to the American entertainment industry in the past 14 years of her career.
