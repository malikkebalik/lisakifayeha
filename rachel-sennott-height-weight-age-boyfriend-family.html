    Rachel Sennott Facts

  • Rachel is a dead ringer for actress Emmy Rossum, who is famous for starring as Fiona Gallagher in the American television series, Shameless.

  • Rachel was named in the ‘Six Best Comedians of the Alt Scene’ lists by both Time Out New York and Pop Dust magazines in 2019.
