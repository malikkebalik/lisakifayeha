    Joe Burrow Wiki: - Joe Burrow was born on December 10, 1996, in Ames, Iowa, U.S. He is an American football quarterback for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League (NFL). Burrow played collegiate football at LSU after attending Ohio State, where as a senior he won the 2020 collegiate Football Playoff National Championship and the Heisman Trophy. In the 2020 NFL Draft, the Bengals picked him first overall.
