    The actress was born on October 21, 1949, in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. LaTanya had always been headstrong and hard-working. Being a woman in the 1940s was challenging on its own, being black added more to the hardship. However, Richardson was never fazed by this as she continued her career and achieved success and admiration along the journey. Not just her, most stars in Hollywood will struggle to walk shoulder to shoulder with Samuel Jackson, and having been married to the Hollywood icon, Richardson’s fame and & achievements look lightweight. Nonetheless, it does not change the facts she has accomplished in her own right as an actress. In this article, we will bring everything we know of LaTanya Richardson, the long-term wife of Samuel Jackson.

    Early life

    LaTanya was born into a strict family. She finished high school in Atlanta. She first fell in love with theatre and plays when her high school mentor Georgie Allen took all the kids to the college to show them children’s theatre. Her parents were not very supportive of the acting profession. However, her headstrong self did not let that stop her from achieving her dreams and aspirations. She later completed her BA in theatre from Spelman College.

    She initially wasn’t interested in acting in movies. Instead, she tried her hand at acting in her college’s play of ‘Macbeth .’The theater in Atlanta was widely famous for having produced talented theatre actors. This gave the strong-willed girl a chance to work on various projects.

    Career:

    Her first film role was in ‘Hanging with the Homeboys’ in 1991. Her role got her the admiration she was looking for. She shook the Off-Broadway shows, earning her a great name. She wanted to switch it up a little and auditioned for TV roles. She got her first big TV debut with ‘A man called Hawk’, which ran for a few months. Yet, her commendable acting skills outshined the series. It got her a role in an episode of ‘Law and Order. 

    She was then seen in more tv series such as ‘NYPD Blue,’‘ Civil Wars,’ ‘Earth 2,’ ‘Judging Amy’ and a lot more. Her professional career was at a halt in the 90s.  She was focusing on building a family and settling down with her husband Samuel L. Jackson. Her huge musical break came with the 2003 musical ‘The Fighting Temptation’. The movie was a big success in the eyes of the critics as well as the audiences. It is still considered to be one of the best works of Richardson to date. She played the role of the antagonist, Paulina Pritchett. 

    She bagged the role in a few movies such as the 2009 movie ‘Mother and Child.’ Unfortunately, this became the last movie she appeared in. Going back to her early days, LaTanya is now more focused on Broadway. In 2018, she performed as Calpurnia in To Kill A Mockingbird play on Broadway.

    Awards and Accomplishments: 

    The theatre actress has been nominated for the Tony Award in the category Best Lead Actress in a Play for her performance in ‘A raisin in the Sun’.  She has also been nominated for the Black Reel Award’s Category Outstanding Supporting Actress in 2014. Along with this, the talented actress and activist, has received an award for the Best History Documentary Program or Series in the Canadian Screen Award.

    Net worth :

    LaTanya Richardson has a net worth of about $10 Million. She is a self-made woman and has shown major pride in it. 

    Relationship with Samuel L. Jackson:

    LaTanya is married once and never divorced. She is in a long-term relationship with her husband Samuel L. Jackson. Undoubtedly the pair is one of the strongest running powerhouse couples in Hollywood.

    The couple met back in the ’70s when she was in college and bonded over their mutual love for acting. The couple got married in 1980 and have been together ever since. In over 40 years of marriage, the pair have given birth to their only child Zoe Jackson who is making strides in Hollywood as a producer and director.

    Evidently, many high-profile celebrity couples have gone separate ways, even at times it seemed there is no breaking up in the relationship. Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, Channing Tatum and Jenna Dewan, Kanya West & Kim Kardashian the list goes on and on. As for LaTanya and Samuel Jackson, the pair are no superhumans as they had their ups and downs. But having the back of one another made the marriage long-lasting. In the early ’90s, Samuel was addicted to drugs and LaTanya put faith in him and helped him get into rehab. Since Samuel has been in recovery.

    Opening the reason behind their long-lasting marriage, Samuel said in an Interview with people:

    “In the beginning, we always said the most revolutionary thing that Black people could do was stay together, raise their children with the nucleus of having a father and a mother, since everybody likes to pretend that that’s not the dynamic of the African American family. That it’s just children out here being raised by women, which we know is false. In order to change that narrative, we made a decision to say, ‘We are going to stay together no matter what. We’ll figure it out.’” 

    And the formula has worked out between the picture-perfect pair who have been married for 42 years and counting. The pair have a specific rule regarding family time, Samuel, is a man in demand in Hollywood, but despite his work commitments, the pair do stop working in July to spend time with their daughter and friends.

    This beautiful broadway star stands at 5 feet 4 inches. She has long dark Ebony hair with alluring and enticing dark brown eyes. Her other measurements such as bust, waist, hips, and shoe sizes are not available.  As a Libra born she is said to possess qualities like charming, beautiful, and well-balanced according to astrological reading.
