    While prepping with her glam squad for the Creative Arts Emmy awards on September 12, Heidi was too excited to share her process with her thousands of fans and admirers. And why shouldn’t she? She looks beautiful 24/7! But her Instagram contained something she appeared not to notice — a nip slip on her left breast, right above the corset top of her gorgeous gown. Omg!

    “Last minute touch ups for Creative Arts #Emmys @cwoodhair@lindahaymakeup @missnewbark@lorraineschwartz @versace_official,” she captioned the black-and-white pic, which showed her getting touched up by her hair stylist and makeup artist. Oh, how we wish we had a team like that to follow us around!

    Heidi looks totally on point, but her exposed breast is all most commenters could pay attention to when looking at the pic. The fact that it’s still up probably means that Heidi doesn’t care! Hey, she does have a history of racy Instagram pics.

    Can we talk about that gown, though? The photo is black and white, so we’re not sure about the color, but her frock is lacy, and boned to look like a corset on top. It’s romantic and dreamy; we would die to get a dress like that! Add to that some lovely appliqué flowers, and it’s just perfect.
