    Meghan Markle: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

    Meghan Markle was born in Los Angeles, California, U.S. on August 4, 1981.

    Meghan spent her early life and childhood with her sister named Samantha Grant and a brother named Thomas Markle Jr.
