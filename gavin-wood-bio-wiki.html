    Who is Gavin Wood?

    Gavin Wood is a British computer scientist and tech entrepreneur. He is known as a co-founder of Ethereum, the world’s second-largest cryptocurrency platform.

    Gavin is also the creator of Polkadot and Kusama. He has left an indelible mark on the blockchain and cryptography landscape.

    Gavin Wood – Professional Life, Careers

    Gavin Wood began his career as a research scientist at Microsoft. In 2013, Wood teamed up with Vitalik Buterin and some other partners to create Ethereum, which made a huge impact in the world of blockchain. Ethereum’s main innovation was the introduction of smart contracts, which are contracts that run automatically based on predetermined conditions.

    Furthermore, his contribution was the creation of the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM), a virtual machine that is Turing-complete and enables developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) on the Ethereum blockchain.

    Similarly, Parity Technologies was founded by Gavin in 2016. This software development company specializes in creating infrastructure and applications for the decentralized web. One of their most notable projects is Polkadot. Polkadot is a multi-chain platform that promotes interoperability between various blockchains, solving scalability and connectivity problems.

    Moreover, Kusama was also created by Gavin. Kusama was launched in 2019 and allows developers to experiment, test, and deploy early-stage projects in a live environment. This ensures that the infrastructure of Polkadot remains strong and safe.

    In conclusion, Gavin Wood is a computer scientist and tech entrepreneur. He is a forward-thinking computer scientist and tech innovator who has established himself as a frontrunner in creating decentralized platforms.
