    Kingston High School Obituary, Death –Tragedy struck Kingston High School on Monday when two students lost their lives in a devastating crash on state Route 28 at around 8:07 a.m., as reported by school authorities. The identities of the students involved in the accident have not been disclosed. State police are actively investigating the serious crash that occurred in Shandaken, leaving the community in shock and sorrow. Schools Superintendent Paul Padalino released a statement addressing the heartbreaking incident, expressing condolences for the loss of two Kingston High School students and acknowledging that another student is currently hospitalized.

    Padalino conveyed the deep sorrow felt by the school community and extended love and support to all those affected by this unimaginable tragedy. Recognizing the emotional toll such events can take, he emphasized the availability of the high school’s Critical Incident Stress Management (CISM) Team. Comprising social workers, psychologists, nurses, counselors, and safety personnel, this team is trained to assist students, staff, and families during traumatic times.

    The CISM Team has been deployed and will remain available throughout the day in all school buildings to provide support, understanding, and comfort to those grappling with the profound loss. The entire Kingston community mourns the tragic loss of these young lives, and the school district is committed to assisting everyone affected during this difficult time.
