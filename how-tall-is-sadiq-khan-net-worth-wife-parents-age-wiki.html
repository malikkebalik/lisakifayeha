    Sadiq Khan Wikipedia

    Sadiq Khan Wikipedia: - Sadiq Khan is a British politician. He was born Sadiq Aman Khan on 8 October 1970 in Tooting, London, England. Since 2016, he has been mayor of London. Between 2005 to 2016, he served as Tooting's Member of Parliament (MP). Before being chosen as the MP for Tooting in the 2005 general election, Khan served from 1994 to 2006 as a councilor for the London Borough of Wandsworth.

    He publicly criticized a number of Labour Prime Minister Tony Blair's policies, such as the 2003 war in Iraq and new anti-terror laws. He was appointed as the Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Communities and Local Government under Gordon Brown, Blair's successor, in 2008. He then rose to the position of Minister of State for Transportation. Following his victory over Conservative candidate Zac Goldsmith in the 2016 London mayoral election, Khan resigned from his position as an MP. In May 2021, he was re-elected mayor. He was included on the 2018 Time 100 list of the world's most influential people.
