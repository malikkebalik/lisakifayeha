    Moo Snuckel Facts

  • He hates spiders and referred to them as “godless killing machines”.
  • When he 1st created a channel on May 29, 2011, he chose the username BnBENTERPR1SES. He later thought it was stupid, but when he picked it, he was a big fan of the TV show The Wire (2002-2008), where it originated from.
  • His nickname, Moo Snuckel, is a wordplay for “Moose Knuckle”, a word given to ‘male cameltoe.’ He found it hilarious at 1st but as his subscriber count grew, he realized it was a tad inappropriate and reduced the username to just Moo.
  • One of his favorite memories was feeding a bear cub in mid-2016.
  • Moo Snuckel is a member of the group of gamers and collaborators on YouTube, referred to as the Vanoss Crew. It consists of VanossGaming, Mini Ladd, I AM WILDCAT, Daithi De Nogla, BigJigglyPanda, SMii7Y, BasicallyIDoWrk, and fourzer0seven.
  • Out of all members of the Vanoss Crew, Moo Snuckel was the 1st to meet and collaborate with VanossGaming. They both had about 50 subscribers at the time and he was the one that sent the 1st direct message.
  • According to him, fourzer0seven has the funniest laugh.
  • Moo Snuckel has had a tough time spelling Charlie Chaplin, the name of a well-known comedian and actor, father of Geraldine Chaplin and grandfather of Oona Chaplin.
  • His YouTube channel’s tagline is “Average at video games, great at having fun!”
  • In collaboration with the brand Soft Serve Toys, he designed and produced a 6-inch-tall vinyl figure of himself in November 2019.
  • One of his daughter’s favorite movies is Brave (2012).
  • His sister has a hedgehog named Poppy.
  • His 1st priority in a female is her personality.
  • He is not the type of person that likes to take pictures of himself. This was one of the reasons he decided not to vlog or do face-cam live streams despite being constantly asked.
