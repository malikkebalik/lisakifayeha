    Christmas came a day early for the BTS army. On December 24, Grammy-nominated group BTS’ Jimin dropped a new festive special track ‘Christmas Love’ on YouTube that garnered more than 4,50,000 views within half an hour of its release. After the surprise track dropped, the 25-year-old K-pop star took to Twitter to share it with fans with a short message for fans, “Everyone’s (Christmas) gift has arrived.”

    And now  BTS Army worldwide is celebrating the 3 minutes 17 seconds long soft-pop track with Jimin’s healing vocals wrapped in Christmasy warmth. 

    The songs' lyrics state: “Oh I been waitin' for this Christmas Day, my heart is already on this white street, Last night, snow fell in my dreams. You waited all day in the blanket… Christmas I love you, Oh you're the one, I hope it will be today forever, Christmas love, Christmas love Stay in my heart for a long time”. These words are enough to encapsulate the ‘Promise’ singer’s feelings for his fans and to top this gift, Jimin also wrote a letter.

    In the Christmas letter, Jimin opened up his heart and said, “Hello everyone, this is Jimin. Did you get a good Christmas present? I really wanted to hear from you, but fortunately, I am very, very happy to be able to do so. The reason I came here today is why I suddenly heard this bright song in this difficult situation, I came here because I wanted to tell you a little about the reason. As you can see from the contents of the song. This song is a scene where my favorite snow falls out of my childhood memories. I sing with my feelings when I first saw them.”

     
    The ‘Friends’ crooner went on and said, “As we grow up, it seems that we miss our childhood a lot. Whether that’s the situation now or not, I thought I wanted to go back to that time. The responsibility of something you have as you grow, That responsibility makes us mature. I thought that it might make us hide our emotions a little.” 
     
    Jimin, known as the comforting angel, said, “All of you are always worthy of love. So, even if it’s a little shy than the usual expression. I hope there will be a situation where everyone can enjoy together. It’s a song that’s far short of what’s written in grandiose, I hoped that all of you would be happy people and made it hard with one wish. I hope I can go back to the old days that you remember a little bit after listening to the song.” 

    Here’s how you can live stream the song

    Jimin’s Christmas Love is available to stream on YouTube, YouTube Music, and his letter to fans is attached to Soundcloud along with the track. 

    What’s the BTS Army’s reaction?

    Within 10 minutes of ‘Christmas Love’ dropping, the ‘Filter’ crooner started trending on Twitter with thousands of fans expressing love and thanking the BTS member for the most thoughtful Christmas present. Here’s what the fans are saying. A fan posted, “You're the best gift ive received this Christmas.”  Another similar tweet read, “He doesn’t know that he is the present for all of us.” A touched fan shared, “Jimin's sweet and healing voice is everything. this song is so precious; so proud of him.”
