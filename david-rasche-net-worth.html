    David Rasche Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

    David Rasche was born on August 7, 1944 in St. Louis, Missouri, USA. He is an actor, known for Burn After Reading (2008), Men in Black 3 (2012) and Sledge Hammer! (1986). He has been married to Heather Lupton since 1979. They have three children. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
