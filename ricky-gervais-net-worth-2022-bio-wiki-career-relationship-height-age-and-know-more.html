    Ricky Gervais Bio/wiki

    Ricky Gervais, along with his three siblings, was born in Reading and grew up in Whitley. During the Second World War, his father moved from Canada to England.

    His father worked in the construction industry while his mother cared for him and his siblings at home. His mother is frequently credited with his wit and humor.

    He worked as a gardener at the University of Reading after graduating from high school before enrolling in University College London. He wanted to major in biology but switched to philosophy and received a second-class honors diploma.

    He met Jane Fallon while attending university, and the two have been together since 1982.
