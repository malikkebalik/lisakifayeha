    Cash Outlaw’s fatal cause

    Cash Outlaw’s death has dealt a severe blow to the Outlaw family. Yet, her true cause of death is unknown. She was admitted to Arkansas Children’s Hospital a few weeks before her death, according to reports.

    Her obituary states that she was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes at the age of two and a half. It is still unknown whether diabetes played a role in her passing.

    Outlaw, one of Chase Outlaw’s six children, was born in 2011. Cash was shared by Chase’s ex-girlfriend Brett Ann Riddle. Hayes and Chloe were the second and third children of his current wife, Nicole.

    She also cherished horses and aspired to become a stunt rider. Cash was intended to be used to attend college and earn a degree in Ranch Management.
