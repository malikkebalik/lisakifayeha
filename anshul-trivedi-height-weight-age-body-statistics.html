    Anshul Trivedi is an Indian actor, singer, and musician who has worked on several shows such as Sasural Genda Phool, Love by Chance, Main Lakshmi Tere Aangan Ki, Kaali – Ek Punar Avatar, Kaun Hai?, Saraswatichandra, Ishq Ka Rang Safed, Sasural Simar Ka, Ek Rishta Aisa Bhi, Khidki, Adaalat, Khoob Ladi Mardaani… Jhansi Ki Rani, Balika Vadhu 2, Guilty Minds, and Trideviyaan. The actor has also been cast in movies like Ramaiya Vastavaiya, Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela, Oxygen, and Pela Adhi Akshar.

    Education

    Anshul Trivedi attended the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda (formerly known as Baroda College) and graduated with a Bachelor’s in Business Administration (B.B.A) degree. He then completed his post-graduation in Public Relations & Corporate Communications (P.R.C.C) from Xavier’s Institute of Communications (XIC) at St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai.

    For 7 years, he received training in Hindustani Shastriya Sangeet (Indian classical).

    Anshul Trivedi Facts

  • He spent his childhood years growing up in Vadodara, Gujarat.
  • For 3 consecutive years, Anshul Trivedi emerged as the winner of the state-level swimming competitions as well as has represented Gujarat at the National level swimming championship organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports.
  • In 2004, he won the Sugama Sangeetha (Light Vocal) competition at National Youth Festival (India).
  • Music legend Viju Shah heard him singing at a competition and offered him to come down to Mumbai and perform with him in Navratri shows and other events. Anshul then went on to work with Viju Shah for more than 5 years.
  • Anshul Trivedi got placed at CMCG India after he finished his post-graduation. However, he quit the job before the completion of the probation period in order to pursue his passion for acting.
  • In 2013, he was cast as Pramadan Dharmadhikari (Kumud’s ex-husband) in the Star Plus soap opera Saraswatichandra.
  • He made his first theatrical film appearance as Anshul (Rhea’s husband) in the 2013 Hindi-language romantic drama film Ramaiya Vastavaiya, a remake of Prabhu Deva‘s directorial debut film Nuvvostanante Nenoddantana. It stars Girish Kumar, Shruti Haasan, and Sonu Sood.
  • Anshul Trivedi brought to life the character of Mandar in the 2013 Hindi-language tragic romance film Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela (or simply known as Ram-Leela), starring Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh.
  • In 2021, he started playing the recurring role of Khimji Bhujaria (Premji’s best friend) in the social drama television series Balika Vadhu 2.
