    In Memoriam: A somber farewell to Jessie Lee Ward, the luminary ambassador and corporate executive of Prüvit, whose indomitable spirit illuminated the world of direct selling. Despite a valiant battle against colon cancer, Jessie Lee Ward’s legacy lives on, transcending her untimely departure on September 16, 2023.

    Jessie Lee Ward Obituary

    A funeral service will be held at All Saints Episcopal Church, 106 W. Church Street, Frederick, Maryland, on October 21 at 2 p.m. Any donations should be made to All Saints Episcopal Church. Jessie Lee Ward (nee O’Sullivan)

    Industry leaders, including Brian Underwood (Founder and CEO of Prüvit), Blake Mallen (President of Prüvit), Ed Mylett (Bestselling Author and Podcast Host), Al Bala (CEO of Mannatech), and Sarah Shadonix (Founder and CEO of Scout & Cellar), share poignant reflections on Jessie Lee’s impact.

    Early Life and Education

    Jessie Lee Ward graced the world on October 12, 1987, in Middletown, Maryland. Her journey into the realms of marketing and communication began at Hunter College in New York City, laying the foundation for her future brilliance in the direct selling arena.

    Rapid Rise at Prüvit

    Joining Prüvit in 2015, Ward rapidly ascended the company’s ranks, earning the distinguished titles of top earner and recruiter. Her unparalleled success manifested in a global network marketing team, uniting 1.6 million customers and 21,000 promoters across 41 countries.

    Entrepreneurship and Mentorship

    Jessie Lee Ward wasn’t just a corporate executive; she was a thriving entrepreneur, author, and influential speaker. Her entrepreneurial prowess extended to coaching and mentoring aspiring network marketers and social media influencers. Notable works include “The MLM Queen” and “Boss Up.”

    Social Media Sensation

    With over a million subscribers across social media platforms, Ward was a digital powerhouse. Her podcast, “The People’s Mentor,” amassed over 10 million downloads, showcasing her insights on business, leadership, mindset, and motivation.

    Conclusion

    Jessie Lee Ward, a legend in the direct-selling arena, leaves an indelible mark. Her uniqueness, drive, and power were unparalleled, making her a hero, an icon, and a shining example for many. As we remember her, we also celebrate the incredible force she was and the brilliance she brought to the world.
