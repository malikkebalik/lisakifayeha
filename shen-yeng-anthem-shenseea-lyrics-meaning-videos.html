    Overall Meaning

    In "Shen Yeng Anthem," Shenseea asserts her power as a confident woman who will not be intimidated by other women. She warns others that she is not afraid to take their men if they do not treat them right, but she also does not want to fight over a man. Shenseea shows that she values herself and her own happiness over just being with a man. She also expresses her sexuality in the song, noting that she has good sex and is not afraid to share it with those she chooses. The lyrics overall convey a message of strength, independence, and self-assuredness, important qualities for anyone to have, but especially for young women.


    The chorus of the song repeats the phrase "No gyal cya see mi and start problem, Dem a man clown so mi walk pon dem." This speaks to the idea that other women are not her competition, and any negativity coming from them is not worth her time or attention. She also refers to herself as a "ShenYeng," which is a term that she has coined to refer to her fanbase and followers.
