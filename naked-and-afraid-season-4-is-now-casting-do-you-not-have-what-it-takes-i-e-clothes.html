    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    If you think a great first date is going au naturel and grubbing up on actual grubs before retiring to a nice, romantic, mostly dry spot beneath some palm fronds, then Discovery Channel wants you! The network is currently casting for the fourth season of America’s favorite nude survivalist reality series, Naked and Afraid.

    The show’s production company, Metal Flowers Media, posted the casting poster above (with a little less censoring) earlier today on Facebook along with the following announcement, which includes a link to apply:

    #NOWCASTING DISCOVERY CHANNEL CALLING ALL SERIOUS SURVIVALISTS FOR ITS HIGHEST RATED SURVIVAL SERIES “NAKED AND AFRAID”

    Are you an expert at primitive survival skills? Could you start a fire by friction? Hunt without a rifle? If you have the skills & mental toughness to survive what may be the most harrowing 21 days of your life, now is your chance to prove it. In this extreme cutting-edge survival series, you will be paired with a partner and survive off-the-grid, stripped of bare necessities including your own clothes. You will rely on one another’s skills and mental fortitude to survive.

    If you have what it takes, please apply:
    www.metalflowersmedia.com/application
