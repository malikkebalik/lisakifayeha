    HOUSTON, TEXAS: 'America's Got Talent' brings talents of all ages, sex, color, and race. It's sometimes astonishing to see how skilled some of the young performers are, often outshining adult competitors. 'AGT' Season 18's auditions continue but this will be the final and last round for hopefuls to bag a spot to compete for the $1M prize money.

    The judges Simon Cowell, Howi Mandel, Sofia Vergara, and Heidi Klum have each had one chance to use their Golden Buzzer on the act of their choice. Until now, all four judges have used the golden buzzer opportunity. But, since Episode 18 marks the end of the audition round, the judges have once again been given one golden buzzer 'save' for a lucky contestant.

    Who is Eduardo Antonio Trevino?

    Eduardo Antonio Trevino is a 10-year-old mariachi prodigy from Houton, Texas. He might be small in stature but he is a big performer in terms of accolades and experience. He is quite well-known in the world of mariachi performers. He has won various prizes for his extraordinary talent, including the top prize at the national mariachi competition. He was also placed 1st in elementary/middle school at the 28th Annual Mariachi Extravaganza in San Antonio. 

    Also Read: 'That was so dangerous': 'AGT: Fantasy League' viewers 'creeped out' by Vardanyan Brothers' balancing act on NBC show

    The journey to stardom began at a very young age for Eduardo. He started singing and performing when he was just 2 years old. He started playing guitar during the pandemic. Eduardo has also bagged state recognition from his District State Representative, Charles Cunningham. Eduardo performs at various places and is often seen posting videos of himself playing the guitar on social media. Eduardo recently performed at the Minute Maid Park on Houston Astros Kids Day.

    Eduardo Antonio Trevino in 'AGT' Season 18

    Eduardo Antonio Trevino will be surprising the judges with his exceptional skills. The young talent will come on the stage with a band. Wearing a blue suit, Eduardo looked astonishing as well as lavish while performing on the 'AGT' stage. He uploaded his picture with the band, adding the song 'Best Day of My Life' to the post. It is to be seen whether Eduardo will be the one for whom the judges will press the golden buzzer.
