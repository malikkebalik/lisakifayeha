    Diane Gilman is a well-known American fashion designer and television personality who dressed America’s rock icons in the 1960s. She is also well-known for her appearance on HSN under the moniker “Jean Queen” (Home Shopping Net Worth). She has a sizable net worth and earns a sizable salary as a result of her career.

    Diane Gilman- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

    Diane Gilman- Professional Career

    Diane Gilman began her business making jeans for celebrities after discovering that her neighbors were members of the rock and roll band Jefferson Airplane.

    She moved to New York in her 80s to launch her line, Cabal, which was picked up and produced by Bloomingdales. Later, in 1994, she began working for HSN, where she celebrated her 20th anniversary in 2014.

    Gilman presently sells her line of jeans, tops, and sandals on HSN, with a focus on providing ladies with comfortable and casual apparel. Her brand is currently the network’s best-selling product.
