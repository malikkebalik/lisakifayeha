    Creadel “Red” Jones Net Worth: $500 Thousand

    Creadel Red Jones was an American singer and original member of the recording group The Chi-Lites. He had a net worth of $500 thousand at the time of his death in August 1994.

    Born in September 1940, Jones was known for his deep bass voice which became a trademark of the group. He joined The Chi-Lites when it formed in 1959 and remained with them until 1973, and then again from 1980 to 1988.

    The Chi-Lites released a total of 18 albums, with their debut studio album Give It Away being released in 1969. Their 1972 album A Lonely Man reached #5 on the US R&B chart and #5 on the Billboard 200 chart.

    Their most popular singles “Have You Seen Her” and “Oh Girl” both reached #1 on the US R&B chart, with “Oh Girl” also reaching #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

    Creadel Red Jones passed away on August 25, 1994.
